This quiz has been created in order to educate newcommers to the hobby of FPV and quad copters. Whilst FPV is a fun and rewarding hobby, it has a notoriously steep learning curve. There is a lot to learn about a wide variety of topics and you must be familiar with them all in order to be a safe and competenet pilot. There are many laws in the UK that restrict flight and it is imperative that you are as familiar with the law as you are with your kit. This quiz aims to highlight some of the key areas of knowledge to focus on and help beginners identify their weaknesses.
The knowledge required to answer the questions within the quiz can be gained from the resouces page, found in the navigation bar, as well as through other online resources. Flying RC FPV quad copters requires patience, skill and often a lot of research and tinkering in order to get this working the way that they should.
Please use this quiz as a basic guide to key knowledge that is required in order to make the most out of the hobby.
